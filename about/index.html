About

This site is run by me — Tyler Hulett. These days, I primarily work on discovering new types of autoimmunity as CSO at CDI Labs. Of my many roles there, I'm helping build The Cancer Antibody Atlas – an international initiative to map autoantibodies and adaptive immunity as it relates to cancer.

Originally taught myself timelapse photography and soundscape recordings as a graduate student. Forgot to renew my original '.org' portfolio domain back in 2020 and today felt like time to rebuild my portfolio.

Discover Oregon started as an effort to recreate what it feels like to be in a few of my favorite offline places. Now expanding into apps, starting with Frog It! – a dice game beloved by my maternal grandmother.
