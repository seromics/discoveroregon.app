About

This site is run by me — Tyler Hulett, PhD. I'm a 5th generation Oregonian, scientist, and part-time filmmaker based in Portland.

By day, I work on antibody research at CDI Labs. By night (and weekends), I build apps and capture Oregon's landscapes through timelapse and video.

Discover Oregon started as an effort to recreate what it feels like to be in a few of Oregon's most beautiful places. Now expanding into apps, including Frog It! — a family dice game based on my grandmother's house rules.
