A Classic Dice Game with a Strategic Twist

Roll & Score

Roll five dice up to three times per turn. Hold the dice you want to keep and try to complete scoring categories like Three of a Kind, Full House, Large Straight, and more!

Bank Your Frogs

Here's the twist: unused rolls become "frogs" that you can save for later! Score early to bank frogs, then spend them when you really need that extra roll.

Go for SPLASH!

Roll all five dice matching for a SPLASH! (Five of a Kind). Your first scores 50 points, and each additional SPLASH earns 100 bonus points!

Compete Worldwide

Submit your best scores to the global leaderboard. Compete in weekly tournaments and daily wildcard challenges with randomized rules!

The Frog Banking Mechanic

Every turn, you get 3 free rolls. But what happens when you score after just 1 or 2 rolls? Those unused rolls become frogs that you bank for later!

Need an extra roll on a crucial turn? Tap "Frog It!" to spend a banked frog and roll again. It's a blend of luck and resource management that transforms the classic dice game!

Multiple Ways to Play

Solo Play

Practice your strategy and aim for high scores. Games with standard tournament rules are eligible for trophies!

Local Multiplayer

Pass the device and compete with 2-4 players. Each player has their own scorecard and frog bank.

Weekly Tournament

Play 3 games in a row - your combined total is your tournament score. One attempt per week!

Daily Wildcard

Each day features random rule variations. Different rolls per turn, starting frogs, and more!
