How It Works

Roll & Score

Roll five dice up to 3 times per turn. Tap any die to hold it (won't change on next roll). Fill all 13 categories to complete the game.

Bank Your Frogs

Score early to bank unused rolls as frogs. Need an extra roll later? Tap "Frog It!" to spend a banked frog and roll again!

Go for SPLASH!

Roll all five dice matching for a SPLASH! (Five of a Kind)
