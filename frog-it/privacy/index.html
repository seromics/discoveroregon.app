Frog It! Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 12, 2026

Overview

Frog It! ("the App") is developed by Discover Oregon Video Productions, LLC. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This policy explains what limited data we collect and how it's used.

Data We Collect

No account required. All submissions are anonymous. When online features are enabled, we collect only:

  • Device Identifier: An anonymous hash of your device, used solely to identify your scores on leaderboards
  • Display Name: The username you choose, shown on public leaderboards
  • Scores: Your game scores for leaderboard display

That's it. We do not collect your name, email, phone number, location, or any other personal information. We have no way to identify you personally.

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively for:

  • Displaying your scores on global leaderboards
  • Participating in weekly tournaments and daily challenges

Data Retention

Leaderboard data (scores and usernames) is retained indefinitely.

Third-Party Services

We use Firebase (Google) to store leaderboard data. Firebase's privacy policy: firebase.google.com/support/privacy

The app displays ads via Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device-level data for ad personalization according to Google's advertising policies. This data collection is handled by Google, not by us. See: policies.google.com/privacy

Your Choices

You can disable online features entirely in the App's Settings. When disabled, no data is sent to our servers and you can play completely offline. Note: this does not delete previously submitted scores.

To request deletion of your leaderboard data, email support@discoveroregon.app with your display name and we will remove your scores.

In-App Purchases

Purchases are processed by Apple and Google. We do not collect or store any payment information.

Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. The latest version will always be available at this URL.

Contact

Questions? Contact us at: support@discoveroregon.app
